Every restaurant I've been to in Pittsburgh.

After being nudged to do so by Trevor Kann, here's a tier list of every restaurant I've been to in the Pittsburgh area, minus obvious chain restaurants you would find anywhere.

Includes places I've dined in only, since takeout does not represent a true dining experience. I'll make some exceptions for places that are predominantly takeout. Results adjusted for cost and grad student budget. Tiers subject to change after subsequent visits.

Tiers are defined as follows:

  • S-Tier: The place really stands out. It's a place Pittsburgh should be proud of.
  • A-Tier: I'd happily go here most days and would recommend it to friends.
  • B-Tier: Place is solid. I would go, but I'm not particularly enthusiastic.
  • C-Tier: Place is fine, a bit inconsistent. Give or take.
  • D-Tier: I generally wouldn't go here again. Peer-pressure required.
